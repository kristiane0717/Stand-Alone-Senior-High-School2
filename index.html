"Good day, future leaders and SASHSANS! Are you ready to take the first step toward achieving your dreams? At STAND ALONE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL, we empower students like you to uncover your passion and unlock your potential. We proudly offer a variety of Senior High School strands tailored to fit your unique interests and aspirations.



For aspiring professionals and innovators, the Academic Track has something for everyone. Whether it's Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) for future entrepreneurs, Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) for budding scientists and engineers, or Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) for those with a heart for service and leadership, our programs will equip you with the knowledge and skills to excel in your chosen field.



For the creatively inclined and technically minded, explore the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track. From IT and culinary arts to automotive and agriculture, you'll gain hands-on experience and certifications that can lead directly to a fulfilling career or business opportunity.
