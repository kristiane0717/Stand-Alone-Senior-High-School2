STAND ALONE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL


STEM

The STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) strand in Senior High School focuses on developing skills in scientific inquiry, technological innovation, engineering principles, and mathematical reasoning. Students in this strand are equipped with the knowledge and problem-solving skills needed to pursue careers in fields like medicine, research, computer science, engineering, and data analysis. The curriculum blends theory with hands-on learning, preparing students for higher education and future careers in a rapidly evolving technological world.
