Information and Communication Technology (ICT) offers countless opportunities that revolutionize how we live, work, and interact. In education, ICT enables e-learning platforms, virtual classrooms, and access to digital resources, making learning more engaging and accessible. Businesses benefit significantly from ICT through automation, efficient communication tools, and e-commerce platforms that allow companies to reach global audiences. Similarly, in healthcare, ICT facilitates telemedicine, electronic health records, and advanced diagnostic tools, improving patient care and management.
